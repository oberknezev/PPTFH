Our mission:
To provide leadership and resources to manage and eliminate the destructive consequences of homelessness in Pacific Palisades, to ensure the community is protected, remains safe and to help our homeless people receive compassionate, effective services with access to permanent housing.
Our record:
Since 2016, our results: 63 individuals, or over 72% of the individuals PPTFH has engaged are now off the streets.
